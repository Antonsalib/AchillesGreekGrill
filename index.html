About Achilles Greek Grill

Named after the legendary Greek hero, Achilles Greek Grill embodies strength, tradition, and excellence in every dish we serve. Our passion for authentic Greek flavors runs deep, just like our roots in culinary tradition.

After proudly serving the Sparta community for three years, we've brought our beloved recipes and warm hospitality to Baxter. We combine time-honored Greek cooking techniques with the finest local ingredients to create an unforgettable dining experience.

What makes us special: Fresh ingredients daily, traditional family recipes, beer on tap, and a commitment to making every guest feel like family.

Discover Our Menu

Explore our carefully crafted menu featuring authentic Greek classics like moussaka, souvlaki, and fresh tzatziki, alongside Southern favorites that have made us a local treasure. Each dish is prepared with passion and the finest ingredients.
